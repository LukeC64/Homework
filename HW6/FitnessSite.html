Here Are Some Of The Best Workout Routines Anyone Can Get Started With!

Chest & Triceps Workout (Push Day)

🏋️ Warm-Up (5-10 min)

Arm Circles & Shoulder Mobility – 2 min

Push-ups – 2 sets of 10 (slow tempo)

Lightweight Bench Press or Dumbbell Press – 1-2 sets of 12-15 reps

🔥 Workout (Strength & Hypertrophy Focus)

Chest Exercises

Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets of 5-8 reps (progressive overload)

Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Chest Dips (Weighted if possible) – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Cable or Dumbbell Flys – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Triceps Exercises

Close-Grip Bench Press – 3 sets of 6-10 reps

Skull Crushers (EZ Bar or Dumbbells) – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Rope Tricep Pushdowns – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Overhead Triceps Extensions – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

🏃 Finisher (Optional)

Push-up Burnout Set – As many reps as possible

Triceps Dips (Bodyweight) – 2 sets of max reps

Back & Biceps Workout (Pull Day)

🏋️ Warm-Up (5-10 min)

Jump Rope or Rowing Machine – 2-3 min

Arm Circles & Shoulder Mobility – 2 min

Dead Hangs (Grip Activation) – 2 sets of 20-30 sec

Light Lat Pulldown or Band Pull-Aparts – 1-2 sets of 15 reps

🔥 Workout (Strength & Hypertrophy Focus)

Back Exercises

Deadlifts (Conventional or Romanian) – 4 sets of 5-8 reps

Pull-Ups (Weighted if possible) – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Barbell or Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Seated Cable Rows or T-Bar Rows – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Face Pulls or Reverse Flys – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Biceps Exercises

Barbell or Dumbbell Curls – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Hammer Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Preacher Curls or Concentration Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

🏃 Finisher (Optional)

Pull-Up Burnout Set – As many reps as possible

Banded Curls (High Reps) – 2 sets of 20 reps

Shoulders & Legs Workout (Leg + Push Hybrid Day)

🏋️ Warm-Up (5-10 min)

Jump Rope or Dynamic Leg Swings – 2-3 min

Bodyweight Squats & Lunges – 2 sets of 10 reps

Arm Circles & Shoulder Dislocations (Band Work) – 2 min

Light Shoulder Press or Lateral Raises – 1-2 sets of 15 reps

🔥 Workout (Strength & Hypertrophy Focus)

Leg Exercises

Barbell Squats – 4 sets of 5-8 reps

Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Leg Press or Step-Ups – 3 sets of 12 reps

Hamstring Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Shoulder Exercises

Overhead Press – 4 sets of 6-10 reps

Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Rear Delt Flys – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Face Pulls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

🏃 Finisher (Optional)

Calf Raises – 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Plate Front Raises – 2 sets of 15 reps

Ab Workout (Core Strength & Definition)

🏋️ Warm-Up (5-10 min)

Jump Rope or High Knees – 2-3 min

Dynamic Torso Twists & Side Bends – 2 min

Plank Hold – 1 set of 30-45 sec

🔥 Workout (Core Strength & Hypertrophy Focus)

Upper & Lower Abs

Hanging or Lying Leg Raises – 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Weighted Crunches – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Reverse Crunches – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Core Stability & Endurance

Ab Rollouts – 3 sets of 10 reps

Plank Variations – 3 sets of 30-60 sec

Hollow Body Hold – 2 sets of 30 sec

🏃 Finisher (Optional)

Mountain Climbers – 2 sets of 30 sec

Flutter Kicks – 2 sets of 30 sec
